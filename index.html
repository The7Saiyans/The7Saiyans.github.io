About The Campaign

The world of Terrathadus is under seige by monsters, fiends, villains, and other dark forces. Starting on an island known as Nodroni, we meet our party and their beginning woes.

A group of heroes, chosen by the Guardians of another world, must rise to protect their new home

Soliana Ashera (Sol), is the youngest biological daughter of the Sun and Eclipse Guardians. Kidnapped at a young age, she was raised by a pair of farmers in the Gold Cliffs. Sol endured countless nightmares until she was found by her mother, the Eclipse.

Re'kha fae'hn (Rowan Paige/Rewn), is the elder sister of Sol, also kidnapped at a young age. She was raised by a loving family in Texas until the fire- where she fled to Yesteryear and was raised by a man known as Fylarion Faelthas. Fylarion helped her recover from a horrible injury and took care of her until his passing.

Nakiana Estrata (Natalia/Nat) is the adopted daughter of the Sun and Eclipse. She was raised in Havenhold until its destruction, and was taken in by the GodCaller. Raised to believe in every word, Natalia didn't question the GodCaller and the Awakening until the Moon Guardian reached out to her.

Hope Alana (Alana) is a woman with a deep past. A spouse of the Sun Guardian, she fell to Terrathadus while chasing the Sun down for icecream. She has taken up the druid path in the hopes of returning home one day to go to bed.

Asha Yaelsrin (Asha) is a man with a deep past and a deep drive to keep his family safe. He is a man of many... unusual talents, and is known as the party's cannibal.

Astoni ShadowSeer (Astoni) is the child of the Star Guardian and a dedicated member of the Rap God's clergy. Having found a purpose, Astoni will stop at nothing to uphold their belief in the real Slim Shady.
