The Party's Goals

A few of the party's goals are as follows:

  • Group up with the chosen individuals the Guardians sent
  • Collect the pieces of the Sun Guardian's core to restore their power
  • Stop the Cataclysm from destroying Terrathadus
  • Figure out how to keep the good half of the GodCaller's soul from turning against the party
  • Stop the GodCaller from destroying the Grotto
  • Save Asha's mom from the GodCaller
  • Discover the Verthian ruins under the Grotto
  • Leave the Grotto alive
